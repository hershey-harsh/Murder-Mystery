Suspects - Murder Mystery


John Doe - Suspect 1

John Doe, a wealthy businessman with a mysterious past, claims to have been out of town during the time of the murder. Had a poor business relation with the victim.

Emily Smith - Suspect 2

Emily Smith, a famous actress known for her erratic behavior, was rehearsing for a play with her co-stars. Had a personal conflict with the victim.

Robert Johnson - Suspect 3

Robert Johnson, the loyal butler of the Crimson Mansion for over 20 years, claims to have been serving dinner to the guests. Had a secret grudge against the victim.
